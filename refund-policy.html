Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We want you to love your I Never Left T-Shirt. If something isn't right, we'll make it right — email us at hello@linkist.ai within 14 days of receiving your order.

1. Returns

Because this is a limited-edition drop, we accept returns only in the following circumstances:

  • The item arrived damaged or defective
  • You received the wrong item or size
  • The item does not match its description

To initiate a return, email hello@linkist.ai with your order number and photos of the issue within 14 days of delivery.

2. Exchanges

We offer size exchanges subject to stock availability. If you ordered the wrong size, contact us within 7 days of receiving your order. The item must be unworn, unwashed, and in its original condition.

You will be responsible for the return shipping cost. We will cover shipping for the replacement.

3. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you by email. Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Stripe processing fees are non-refundable.

4. Non-Returnable Items

  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items returned after 14 days of delivery
  • Sale or discounted items (unless faulty)

5. Order Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement by emailing hello@linkist.ai. Once the order has been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled — please follow the return process instead.

6. Contact

For all refund and exchange queries, email us at hello@linkist.ai. Please include your order number and we'll respond within 1 business day.
