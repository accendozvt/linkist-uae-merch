Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Linkist UAE ("we", "us", "our") operates this limited-edition merchandise store at linkist.ai. We are responsible for the personal data you provide when purchasing our I Never Left T-Shirt and other products.

2. Data We Collect

When you create an account or place an order, we collect:

  • Full name and email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Order history and transaction details

We do not collect or store your card details. All payment data is handled directly by Stripe, Inc. on their secure servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfil your order
  • To send order confirmation and shipping updates by email
  • To allow you to track past orders via your account
  • To contact you if there is an issue with your delivery

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate this store:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Supabase — secure database storage
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Vercel — website hosting

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your order data for up to 3 years for accounting and fulfilment purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at hello@linkist.ai.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@linkist.ai.

7. Cookies

This site uses only essential cookies required for the shopping cart and authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, email us at hello@linkist.ai.
